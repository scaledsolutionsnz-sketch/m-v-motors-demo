Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

M V Motors is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you use our website or contact us:

  • Name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Vehicle information (make, model, registration)
  • Service enquiry details and messages
  • Feedback and review content submitted through our forms

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and service requests
  • Provide and manage mechanical services
  • Improve our services based on feedback
  • Contact you about your vehicle or booking

We will not use your information for marketing purposes without your consent, and we will never sell your data to third parties.

3. Storage and Security

Your personal information is stored securely and we take reasonable steps to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Form submissions are processed via StaticForms and delivered directly to our email.

4. Third Parties

We may share your information with third parties only where necessary to provide services (e.g., parts suppliers), and only with your implied or explicit consent. We do not sell or rent your personal data.

5. Your Rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access and correct the personal information we hold about you. To request access or correction, contact us at mvmotors@xtra.co.nz.

6. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. No personally identifiable information is stored in cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at mvmotors@xtra.co.nz or call 03 418 4151.
